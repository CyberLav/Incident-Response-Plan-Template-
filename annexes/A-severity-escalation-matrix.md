# Annex A — Severity Classification and Escalation Matrix

| Severity | Criteria | Escalation | Battle Procedure Compression |
|---|---|---|---|
| SEV1 | Active exploitation, critical system down, confirmed data exfiltration, or regulatory clock started | Immediate exec + legal notification, IC activated within 15 min | Compress to frag-order tempo — verbal orders, written confirmation follows |
| SEV2 | Confirmed compromise, contained blast radius, no active exfiltration confirmed | Exec notified within 1 hr, IC activated within 30 min | Abbreviate detailed estimate; move to containment order faster |
| SEV3 | Suspected compromise, unconfirmed, limited scope | Team lead notified, IC activated within 2 hrs | Full 16-step tempo followed |
| SEV4 | Anomaly requiring investigation, no confirmed compromise | Standard triage queue | Investigation only; full activation not required unless escalated |

> Add jurisdiction-specific regulatory notification timelines here — e.g., breach
> notification clocks, sector-specific requirements (PIPEDA, GDPR, state breach laws, etc.)
