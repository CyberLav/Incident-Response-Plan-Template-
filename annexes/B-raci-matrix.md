## Small Org (1–2 person security function)

| Task | IC | Technical Lead | Scribe | Comms Lead | Exec Sponsor |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Declare incident / severity | A/R | C | I | I | I |
| Execute containment | C | A/R | I | I | I |
| Decision log | I | I | A/R | I | I |
| External comms | C | I | I | A/R | C |
| Authorize business-impacting action | C | C | I | I | A |

*In a 1-person team, one person may hold IC + Technical Lead + Scribe simultaneously 
document this explicitly rather than leaving roles undefined.*
