# Lessons Learned

This phase happens after the incident, not during it. Short by design.

- [ ] Schedule the AAR within 5 to 10 business days
- [ ] Run it using [Annex G](../annexes/G-aar-template.md): what was supposed to happen,
  what actually happened, why the difference, what to sustain, what to improve
- [ ] Capture MTTD, MTTC, MTTE, MTTR
- [ ] Assign owners and due dates to every improvement item
- [ ] Update the plan and the relevant playbook
